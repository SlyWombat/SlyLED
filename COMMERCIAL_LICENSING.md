# Commercial Licensing

SlyLED is distributed under the [PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0](LICENSE), which permits **personal**, **hobbyist**, **educational**, **research**, and **charitable** use free of charge.

**Commercial use requires a separate commercial license.** This page explains what that means and how to arrange one.

## Who needs a commercial license

You need a commercial license if any of the following apply:

- **Paid engagements** — you (or your company) are being paid by a client or employer to design, operate, or install lighting shows built with SlyLED. This includes tour production, event production, venue lighting design, consulting, and system integration services.
- **Corporate / business internal use** — a for-profit company uses SlyLED for its own shows, product demos, trade-show booths, internal training, or any activity that supports a commercial purpose.
- **Embedding in a product** — you distribute SlyLED, or derivative works of it, as part of a commercial hardware or software product you sell or license.
- **Paid services built on SlyLED** — you offer SaaS, hosted playback, remote control, subscription features, or any paid service that uses SlyLED on the backend.
- **Rental / lease** — you rent SlyLED-equipped hardware (moving heads, LED walls, controllers) to paying customers.

You do **not** need a commercial license for:

- Personal use at home, for hobbies, research, learning, religious observance, or private entertainment.
- Use by a charitable, educational, public-research, public-safety, environmental, or government institution.
- Use by a nonprofit organization whose activities do not generate commercial revenue from the SlyLED integration.
- Testing, experimentation, and study, including short-term evaluation to decide whether a commercial license is worth pursuing.

If you're unsure which bucket you're in, ask (contact below). We'd rather clarify than have you guess.

## What a commercial license typically includes

A commercial license is a separate written agreement that grants, in exchange for a fee:

- Rights to use SlyLED for the commercial purposes listed above.
- Rights to distribute SlyLED (or modifications) with your commercial products, subject to source-availability obligations.
- Support and update terms commensurate with the engagement (typically email-tier support for the single-show / single-venue tier; SLA and priority patching for the integrator tier).
- Warranty, liability, and indemnity terms negotiated to the engagement's risk profile (the noncommercial license offers none; a commercial contract does).

We offer pricing tiers for:

1. **Single-show / single-venue** — one named production or installation.
2. **Production company / touring** — a named company, any show they produce in the license term.
3. **Integrator / OEM** — embed SlyLED in commercial products you sell, with distribution rights.
4. **Enterprise** — multi-entity use, custom terms.

Tier pricing is updated at <https://electricrv.ca/slyled/licensing> (follow the link or ask for the current sheet).

## Good-faith compliance

The spirit of the noncommercial license is straightforward: **hobbyists and nonprofits get it free; anyone making money with it pays**. We extend significant good faith to anyone acting in that spirit — if a rolling cash-flow issue makes a licensed tier hard to afford for an upcoming engagement, reach out, we'll work with you.
