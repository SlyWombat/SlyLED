# Licensing

SlyLED is distributed under the [PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0](https://polyformproject.org/licenses/noncommercial/1.0.0/) licence. That gives you a simple split:

## Free

**Personal, hobbyist, educational, research, charitable, and nonprofit use is free.** No registration, no key, no phone-home. Download the installer, build from source, modify it, give it to a friend — all fine under the noncommercial licence. The only obligation is to pass along the licence text and the `Required Notice` copyright line to anyone you distribute it to.

Specifically included in the free tier:

- A hobbyist running a home rig.
- A student learning stage lighting at school.
- A church, synagogue, or mosque lighting their services.
- A community theatre company running an unpaid season.
- A university drama department teaching a class.
- A public library hosting a maker-space workshop.
- A charity fundraiser's one-off event.

## Commercial licence required

**Any use where money is changing hands in connection with SlyLED requires a separate commercial licence.** That includes:

- **Paid engagements.** Lighting designers, integrators, and show operators being paid by a client to run SlyLED on that client's rig.
- **For-profit internal use.** Companies using SlyLED for their own trade shows, product demos, internal training, or ambient-lighting installations.
- **Embedded products.** Distributing SlyLED (or modifications) as part of a commercial product you sell or license.
- **Paid services.** SaaS, hosted playback, subscription features, or any paid service where SlyLED runs on the backend.
- **Rental / lease.** Renting SlyLED-equipped hardware to paying customers.

If you're unsure which bucket you fall into, reach out — we'd rather clarify than have you guess.

## Good-faith compliance

The spirit of the noncommercial licence is: **hobbyists and nonprofits get it free; anyone making money with it pays**. We extend significant good faith to anyone acting in that spirit — if a rolling cash-flow issue makes a licensed tier hard to afford for an upcoming engagement, reach out, we'll work with you.
