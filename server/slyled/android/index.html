Android Companion

Your stage in your pocket. Control the show from anywhere.

← Back to SlyLED

A live operator tool — not an editor. The Android app shows your 3D stage in real-time, lets you start and stop shows with one tap, toggle camera tracking, and aim moving heads by pointing your phone. Connect via QR code or IP and you're running the show.

  • Live stage viewport with fixtures, beam cones, and tracked object markers
  • Pinch-to-zoom and drag-to-pan across the stage
  • One-tap timeline start, playlist control, and show stop
  • Camera tracking toggle — start and stop detection from your phone
  • Pointer Mode — aim moving heads using your phone's gyroscope
  • Device status monitoring: performers, cameras, DMX engine
  • Global brightness slider always within reach
  • QR code or manual IP connection to any SlyLED server
